🌑 Why the Moon Changes Phases

The Moon reflects sunlight. As it orbits Earth, we see different parts of its lit side, creating phases over ~29.5 days.

🌓 Main Phases

  • New Moon
  • First Quarter
  • Full Moon
  • Last Quarter

🌊 Tides

The Moon’s gravity causes ocean tides. Strongest during full and new moons.

🌘 Eclipses

Occur when the Sun, Earth, and Moon align.

✨ Phenomena

Includes supermoons, blue moons, and blood moons.
